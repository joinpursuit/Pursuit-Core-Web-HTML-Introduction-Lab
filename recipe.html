The Perfect PB&J

Description

This peanut butter and jelly sandwhich is my favorite sandwich. It has the perfect balance of ingredients and looks great when made right.

Ingredients

  • two slices of white bread
  • one jar of grape jelly
  • creamy peanut butter
  • butter knife
  • a sharp knife
  • cutting board

Directions

  1. lay both sices of bread next to each other on a cuting board
  2. with the butter knife, spread a 1/8 inch layer of peanut butter on the left side
  3. on the right side, spread a 1/8 inch layer of jelly
  4. carefully place the two halves together so that the jelly is on top
  5. with the sharp knife, carefully cut the sandwich in half
  6. EAT THAT SHIT!!
