The NYC BaconEgg&Cheese

Description

The breakfast sandwich that conquered the Big Apple. No true New Yorker starts their day without a delicious and gooey B.E.C (which is short for Bacon Egg and Cheese)!

Ingredients

  • 1 toasted kaiser roll
  • 3 tablespoons butter
  • 2 slices of crispy bacon
  • 2 organic eggs
  • 2 slices of American cheese
  • Salt and freshly ground pepper to perfection

Directions

  1. Cook eggs and bacon
  2. Toast the kaiser roll until golden crispy
  3. Layer the cheese and the bacon on top of one the eggs and, using a spatula, flip the other egg on top of the bacon.
  4. Cook for a few extra seconds for the cheese to melt.
  5. Once the cheese has melted, transfer the eggs to the kaiseer bottom and top with the bagel top.
  6. Serve immediately and enjoy it!
