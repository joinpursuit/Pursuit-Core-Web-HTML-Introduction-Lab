The Perfect PB&J

Description

This peanut butter and jelly sandwich is my favorite sandwich. It has the perfect balance of ingredients and looks great when it is made right.

Ingredients

  • 2 slices of white bread
  • 1 jar of grape jelly
  • 1 jar of creamy peanut butter
  • A butter knife
  • A sharp knife
  • A cutting board

Directions

This is a link to directions for making a PB&J.
  1. Gather Your Ingredients for the Sandwich
  2. Put Gloves on (optional)
  3. Pull Out Two Slices of Bread
  4. Open Peanut Butter and Jelly
  5. Spread the Peanut Butter Onto One Slice of Bread
  6. Spread the Jelly Onto the Other Slice of Bread
  7. Combine the Two Slices
  8. Cut Off the Crusts (optional)
  9. Cut Into Half
  10. Clean Up Your Workspace
  11. Enjoy Your Sandwich
