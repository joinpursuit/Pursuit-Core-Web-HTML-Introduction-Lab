The Perfect PB&J

Link text

Description

This penut butter and jelly sandwich is my favorite sandwich. It is perfect balace of ingredients and looks great when made right

Ingredients

  • 2 slice of white bread
  • 1 jar of grape jelly
  • A butter knife
  • A sharp knife
  • A cutting borad

Directions

  1. Lay both slices of bread next to each other on a cutting board
  2. With the butter knife, spread a 1/8 inch layer of penut butter on the left side
  3. On the right side, spread a 1/8 inch layer of jelly
  4. Carefully place the two halves together so that the jelly is on top
  5. With the sharp knife, carefully cut the sandwich in half
