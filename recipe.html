The Classic Meatloaf

Description

TThe best meatloaf recipe ever! This traditional meatloaf recipe is just like mom used to make, made with ground beef and a sweet and tangy glaze topping.

Ingredients

  • ground beef
  • dried bread crumbs
  • diced onion
  • milk
  • egg
  • worcestershire sauce
  • salt and pepper

Directions

  1. Mix it together REALLY well
  2. Pour the meat mixture into a loaf pan.
  3. Spread it into a flat even layer.
  4. Add ketchup, brown sugar, and red wine vinegar to a bowl and glaze it over the meatloaf.
  5. Cook in a 350 degree oven, uncovered, for 55 minutes.
