The Perfect PB and J

Description

This is a recipe for the best doggone peanut butter and jelly (aka PBJ) sandwich you WILL ever eat!!!

Ingredients

  • Two slices of bread of your choice
  • Peanut butter
  • Aunt Mae's Jelly

Directions

  1. Lightly toast the bread.
  2. Use a knife to spread the peanut butter on both slices.
  3. Use a knife to spread the jelly atop the peanut butter.
  4. Place the slices of bread together.
  5. ENJOY!!!
