The Perfect PB&J

Description

The most beautiful snadwich made perfect for any time! This would keep one full during tea time with small amount

Ingredients

  • 2 slices of white bread
  • 2 teaspoon of peanut butter
  • 2 teaspoon of strawberry jam
  • Small pitch of brown sugar

Directions

  1. Toast bread
  2. Spred peanut butter on one slice
  3. Spred strawberry on the other slice
  4. Sprinkle brown sugar on peanut butter side
  5. Put bread together
  6. Take bite of bread
