The Perfect PB&J

Description

This peanut butter and jelly sandwich is my favorite sandwich. It has the perfect balance of ingredients and looks great when made right

Ingredients

  • 2 slices of white bread
  • 1 jar of grape jelly
  • 1 jar of creamy peanut butter
  • A butter knife
  • A sharp knife
  • A cutting board

Directions

  1. Lay both slices of bread next to each other on a cutting board.
  2. With the butter knife, spread a 1/8 inch layer of peanut butter on the left side.
  3. On the right side, spread 1/8 inch layer of jelly.
  4. Carefully place the two halves together so that the jelly iso n top.
  5. With the sharp knife, carefully cut the sandwich in half
  6. Eat the right half of the sandwich first, then the left
  7. Optionally, you can cut it in halves, or quarters and diagonally or right down the middle. No judgment here.
  8. ENJOY
