Creamy Overnight Oats

Description

If you’ve never had overnight oatmeal, they’re basically a no-cook method of making oatmeal. So, instead of cooking oatmeal on the stove top or in the microwave, you soak the raw oats with milk.

Ingredients

  • Oats
  • Milk
  • Chai Seeds
  • Yogurt
  • Vanilla extract
  • Sweetener

Directions

  1. To start, combine old fashioned oats and chia seeds. You can use a bowl, a mason jar or just some glass cups.
  2. Next add the yogurt and vanilla extract.
  3. And then add the sweetener of choice and the milk.
  4. Then mix the overnight oats together until you see no clumps.
  5. Next, seal or cover with a lid, and chill in the fridge for at least 2 hours or overnight.
  6. The next morning, grab a spoon, add your favorite toppings and dig in!
