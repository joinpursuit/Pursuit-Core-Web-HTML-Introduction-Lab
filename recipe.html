The Perfect PB&J Sandwich

Description

This peanut butter and jelly sandwich is my favorite sandwich. It has the perfect balance of ingredients.

Ingredients

  • 2 slices of white bread
  • 1 jar of grape jelly
  • 1 jar of creamy peanut butter
  • A butter knife
  • A sharp knife
  • A cutting board
  • A toaster

Directions

  1. Take two slices of bread and toast to your liking.
  2. Afterwards lay both slices of toasted bread next to each other on a cutting board.
  3. With the butter knife, spread a 1/8 inch layer of peanut butter on the left side.
  4. On the right side, spread a 1/8 inch layer of grape jelly.
  5. Carefully place the two halves together so that the jelly is on top.
  6. With the sharp knife, carefully cut the sandwich at a bias or in other words diagonally.
