The Perfect PB&J

Description

The perfect PB & J sandwhich

Ingredients

  • Bread
  • Peanut Butter
  • Jelly
  • optional Banana

Directions

  1. Spread peanutbutter onto one slice
  2. Spread jelly on another slice
  3. Optionally chop bananas and put slices on peanutbutter side
  4. press slices together
