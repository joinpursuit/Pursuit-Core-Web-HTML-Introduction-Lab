The Perfect Grilled Cheese

Description

A delicious and cheesy comfort for these cold winter days!

Ingredients

  • 2 slices of white bread
  • 2 slices each of 2 cheeses of choice
  • Butter
  • Minced garlic
  • Dried parsley flakes

Directions

  1. Assemble cheese as you'd like inside of the slices of toast and sandwich bread together.
  2. Mix butter, garlic and parsley itogether using a fork.
  3. Apply half of the mixture to one side of sandwich.
  4. Put a frying pan on medium-low heat and put sandiwch in mixture side down.
  5. Apply rest of mixture to top side of sandwich. Wait 4 minutes, flip, wait 4 more minutes and you are all done!
