The Perfect PB & J

Description

This peanut butter sandwich is my favorite sandwich. It has the perfect blend of ingredients when made right

Ingredients

  • 2 slices of white bread
  • 1 jar of jelly
  • 1 jar of peanut butter
  • A butter knife
  • A sharp knife
  • A cutting board

Directions

  1. Lay both slices of bread down on cutting board
  2. Use butter knife to spread 1/8 inch layer of peanut butter on one side
  3. Spread 1/8 inch layer of Jelly on the other side
  4. Carefully place the two halves together with jelly on top
  5. Use sharp knife to cut in half
