The Perfect PB&J

Description

This peanut butter and jelly sandwhich is my faorite sandwhich. It has the perfect balance of ingredients and looks great when madde right.

Ingredients

  • 2 slices of white bread
  • 1 jar of grape jely
  • 1 jar of creamy peanut butter
  • A butter knife
  • A sharp knife
  • A cutting board

Directions

  1. Lay bith slices of bread next to eachother on a cutting board.
  2. With the butter knife, spread a 1/8 inch layer of peanut butter on the left side
  3. On the right side, spread a 1/8 inch layer of jelly
  4. Carefully place the two halves together so the jelly us on top.
  5. With the sharp knife, carefully cut the sandwhich in half.
  6. Enjoy
