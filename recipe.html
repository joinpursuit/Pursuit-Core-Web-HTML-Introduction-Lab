Ingredients

  • 2 slice of white bread
  • 1 jar of grape jelly
  • 1 jar of creamy peanut butter
  • A butter knife
  • A sharpt knife
  • A cutting board

Directions

  1. Lay both slices next to each other on a cutting board.
  2. With a butter knife, spread a 1/8 inch layer of peanut butter on the left side.
  3. on the right side, spread 1/8 inch layer of jelly.
  4. Carefully place the two halves together so that jelly is on top.
