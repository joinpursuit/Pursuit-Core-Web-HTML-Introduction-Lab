The Perfect PB&J

Description

This peanut butter and jelly sandwich is my favorite sandwich. It has the perfect balanace of ingredients and looks great when made right.

Ingredients

  • 2 slices of white bread
  • 1 jar of brape jelly
  • 1 jar of creamy peanut butter
  • A butter knife
  • A sharp knife
  • A cutting board

Directions

  1. Lay both sides of bread next to each other on a cutting board.
  2. With a butter knife, spread a 1/8 inch layer of peanut butter on the left side.
  3. On the right side, spread a 1/8 inch layer of jelly.
  4. Carefully place the two halves together so that the jelly is on top.
  5. With a sharp knife, Carefully cut the sandwich in half.
  6. Enjoy the PB&J!
