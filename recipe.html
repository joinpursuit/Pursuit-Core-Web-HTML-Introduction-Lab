A Hearty, Healthy Bowl of Chili

Description

This chili soup recipe will warm you up on the coldest of winter nights.

Ingredients & Supplies

    • Stock-Pot
    • Cutting Board

    • Tomatoes
  • Seasonings
    • Chili Powder
    • Smoked Paprika
    • Salt
    • Ground Cumin
    • Pepper
  • Toppings
    • Shredded Cheese
    • Sour Cream
  • Canned Beans
  • Veggie Stock
  • Vegetable Oil

Directions

  1. Heat a 1/2 cup of vegetableoil in your stockpot.
  2. Saute the onion, followed by the red bell pepper and garlic.
  3. Add remaining ingredients to the pot, Then cook until the mixture reaches a simmer.
  4. Cover pot, and lower the heat a bit to maintain the simmer, cook until the lentils are tender.
  5. You're all done!
