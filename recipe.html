The Perfect Bowl of Cereal

Description

This instruction for the perfect bowl of cereal will change your life. I guarantee you will never find a recipe with a milk to cereal ratio as accurate as mine.

Ingredients

(warning.... this recipe must not contain raisins as raisin brans are NOT CEREAL)

  • Bowl (preferably a circumference equal to the top of your head.)
  • Milk (non of that skim milk... I'm talking whole milk, straight from the Cows udder in your backyard)
  • Cereal (preferably the one with a rabbit in the box)
  • Spoon (not to be confused with a fork)
  • Bib - because if you're reading a recipe on how to make cereal... you need a bib.

Directions:

  1. Clean your bowl. Please clean it you dirty person.
  2. Pour the cereal (*** YOU MUST ALWAYS POUR THE CEREAL FIRST)
  3. Pour the milk and make sure it's 3/4ths of the length of the bowl.
  4. Place bib slowly and carefully on your neck.
  5. Eat your cereal (with your spoon of course.)
  6. Clean your bowl again. stop making your mom do everything.
