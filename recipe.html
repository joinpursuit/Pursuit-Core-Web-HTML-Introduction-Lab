The Perfect PB&J

Description

This peanut butter and jelly sandwhich is my favorite sandwhich. It has the perfect balance of ingredients and looks great when made.

Ingredients

  • 2 slices of white bread
  • 1 jar of grape jelly
  • 1 jar of creamy peanut butter
  • A butter knife
  • A sharp knife
  • A cutting board

Directions

  1. Lay both slices of bread next to each other on a cutting board.
  2. With the butter knife, spread a 1/8 inch layer of peanut butter on the left side.
  3. On the right side, spread a 1/8 inch layer of jelly.
  4. Carefully place the two halves togther so that the jelly is on top.
  5. With the sharp knife, carefully cut the sandwhich in half.
  6. Then enjoy
