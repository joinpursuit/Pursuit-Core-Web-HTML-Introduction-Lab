The Perfect PB&J

Description

A peanut butter sandwich is a classic and should be treated as such

Ingredients

  • 2 slices of bread
  • 2 tbsp of grape jelly
  • 2 tbsp of peanut butter
  • 1 butter knife
  • 1 plate

Directions

  1. lay each slice of bread next to each other on the plate
  2. take the butter knife and spread the jelly on one slice of bread
  3. after that, wipe of the excess jelly off the knife and then spread the peanut butter on the other slice of bread
  4. combine the two slices of bread, having the jelly and peanut butter meet
  5. take the knife and cut the sandwich in half if you prefer, Enjoy :)
