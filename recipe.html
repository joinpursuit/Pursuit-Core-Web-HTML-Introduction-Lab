The Perfect PB&J

Description

This peanut butter and jelly sandwich is my favorite sandwich. It has the perfect balance of ingredients and looks great when made right.

Ingredients

  • 2 slices of white bread
  • 1 jar of grape jelly
  • 1 jar of creamy peanut butter
  • A butter knife
  • A sharp knife
  • A cutting board

    • Directions

    1. Lay both slices of braed next to each other on a cutting board.
    2. With the butter knife, spread a 1/8 inch kayer of peanut butter on the left side.
    3. On the right side, spread a 1/8 inch layer of jelly.
    4. Carefully place the two halves together so that the jelly is on top.
    5. With the sharp knife, carfully cut the sandwich in half.
