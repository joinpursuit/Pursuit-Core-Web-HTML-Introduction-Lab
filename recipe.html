The Perfect PB&J

Description

This peanut butter and jelly sandwich is my absolute favorite. It has the perfect balance of ingredients and always looks amazing when done right.

Ingredients

  • 2 slices, white bread
  • 1 jar, grape jelly
  • 1 jar, crunchy peanut butter (NEVER creamy)

Directions

  1. Lay both slices of bread next to each other on a cutting board.
  2. With a butter knife, spread a 1/8 inch layer of peanut butter on the left side.
  3. One the right side, spread a 1/8 inch layer of jelly.
  4. Carefully place the two halves together so that the jelly is on top.
  5. With a sharp knife, carefully cut the sandwich in half.
  6. Eat sandwich. Don't let it sit, or it will be soggy and sad.
