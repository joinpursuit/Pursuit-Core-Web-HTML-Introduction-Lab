Spaghetti and Meatballs

Description

As a guy on a strict budget who also enjoys delicious food, spaghetti and meatballs are an absolute must in my mealplan

Ingredients

  • 2 Heirloom tomatoes
  • Any kind of pasta(fettuchini is my favorite)
  • Lemon juice
  • Tomato sauce
  • Ground turkey
  • Salt
  • Pepper

Directions

  1. Put water on the fire to boil.
  2. While water is boiling, mash ground turkey with some salt and pepper into meatballs and fry them up in a separate pan
  3. When water is boiling, add pasta alongside a little oil to the water
  4. Once pasta is cooked, drain most of the water, leaving only a little bit in order to cook the tomato sauce.
  5. Pour tomato sauce, cut up tomatoes, and meatballs into pasta, and put back on the fire to cook the entire mixture.
  6. Serve and enjoy!
