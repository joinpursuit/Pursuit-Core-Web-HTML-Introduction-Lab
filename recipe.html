The Perfect PB&J

Description

This peanut butter and jelly sandwich is my favorite sandwich. It has the perfect balance of ingredients and looks great when made right

Ingredients

  • 1 jar of Peanut butter or other nut butter
  • 2 slices of wheat bread
  • 1 jar of organic jelly or jam
  • A butter knife
  • A sharp knife
  • A cutting board

Directions

  1. Spread peanut butter on bread.
  2. Spread jelly on bread.
  3. Add toppings, if desired.
  4. Put slices of bread together and use kitchen scissors to cut in half.
  5. Enjoy!
